# The Great Wall of China by MatthewJames

Watch in HD

The Great Wall of China is the longest man-made structure in the world. It was built over a period of 2000 years, from the 7th century BC to the 17th century AD. It was built to protect China from invasions by nomadic tribes from the north and west. The Great Wall is made of stone, brick, wood and earth. It is 8,850 km long and 5 to 10 metres wide. It is 6 to 9 metres high. The Great Wall is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
